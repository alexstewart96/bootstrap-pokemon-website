Why I still like pokemon

I still like pokemon because the games always seem to evolve and keep getting intersting plots and story lines and the quality of the series has always been very good I also just like the fact that you can pick it up and play instantly and it doesn't have the same issues that most rpg,s have (takes forever to get anywhere you have to farm for items or experience).
