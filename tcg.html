The cards

Pokemon cards were first manufactured in 1996 for 1998 for America and 1999 for Europe they where a huge success due to its simple fun to play merchanics and it had a massive worldwide shortage which caused pokemon card prices to shoot through the roof with the cards going for hundreds of pounds but this also caused a mini crimewave which caused many schools to ban the game. these days the tcg has had a mini revival and with the pokemon generations set it is great that they are finally reprinting old cards that appeal to collectors and classic enthusiatsts
