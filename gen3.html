Pokemon generationsthe different eras of pokemon

Gen 3 major changes

Gen 3 brought more major changes to the series with the graphics no longer 8 bit they looked like super nintendo graphics which for the handheld games was a major improvement unfortunately they removed the day and night cycle and the ability to trade between the older games and the sales were not as good as expected signalling the end of pokemainia. This was the generation when we moved from square pixels to something that resembles a modern game and the nintendo gamecube allowed 3d graphics that had never been seen before in pokemon which where amazing for the time.

pokemon ruby and sapphire and emerald

Pokemon ruby and sapphire changed the story quite a lot which in my opinion was very important that they got rid of team rocket and replaced them with team aqua and magma the two best criminal organisations in the series and it introduced weather effects which were cool at the time and the introduction of abilities and double battles did improve battling overall.

pokemon colosseium and xd gale of darkness

These where the two pokemon games that are amazing graphicaly and gameplay wise quite a rare combination. Pokemon colosseum and xd gale of darkness where two 3d RPG games for the nintendo gamecube when i first saw screenshots for colosseum i was just amazed at the graphics they were by far the best pokemon graphics i had ever seen and when I played it wow the rock metal themed guitar music was just incredible it still is one of the best pokemon soundtracks ever it is a very hard game (i have come close but never beaten either of them) and the layout of the towns can be frustrating with lots of odd turns but still the game's amazing

other pokemon games
