Gen 2 the upgrade

This was the first generation after the originals so it was widely anticipated although there where fears about whether the games would be as good as the originals since most sequels where never as good as the originals pokemon bucked the trend and created one of the greatest pokemon games of all time were they managed to get everything right.

pokemon gold and silver and crystal

Pokemon gold ,silver and crystal where in my opinion the best sequel ever made to a gaming series since it improved in every way over the original games and it still kept what was good about the originals my favourite new feature was the day to night transition which at the time on the gameboy color was just incredible how they managed it and they had 2 whole regions in a single cartridge!.

pokemon stadium 2

Pokemon stadium 2 was the only other pokemon game released during gen 2 but it it improved on the graphics of the original stadium as well as having all the same features but with gen 2 added pokemon it also had earl’s academy which was fun tactical battles where you had to use a certain move (endure then reversal) the only thing it was lacking was the original stadium minigames although stadium 2 does have minigames it's a shame that they didn’t port the original 9 minigames as well.

other pokemon games

There was only one other pokemon game during this generation pokemon puzzle challenge basically its puzzle league in handheld form.
